KEY FIX: POST-OVERLAY GAMMA CORRECTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: v18 overlays added semi-transparent
black bars (alpha 0.6) which darkened the
image AFTER the gamma pipeline had already
targeted 0.45 brightness. Result: BirdEye
exposure scores dropped to 18, 34, 49.

Fix: Apply gamma_exposure(0.45) as the LAST
step, AFTER all overlays are composited.
Then apply light USM(80%) to restore any
sharpness softened by the gamma correction.

Impact per camera:
  scene1_BirdEye: 84→84 (exposure was ok)
  scene2_BirdEye: 87→90 (+3)
  scene3_BirdEye: 84→89 (+5) ← biggest gain
  scene4_SecurityCam: 93→100 (+7) ← perfect!

Lesson: Gamma correction must ALWAYS be the
final step in any pipeline that adds overlays
or composites. The brightness target (0.45)
must account for ALL image modifications.
