WHAT THE BUILDER DOES

  For each scene:
    [1] Loads v11 source file
    [2] Applies subdivision surface (level 2) → smooth geometry
    [3] Creates & assigns PBR materials (vehicle, glass, rubber, asphalt)
    [4] Configures lighting (Nishita sky for day, sodium vapor for night)
    [5] Adds forensic exhibit overlay (case number, disclaimer bars)
    [6] Places evidence markers (colored cones at impact zones)
    [7] Configures EEVEE NEXT rendering (1920×1080, 64 samples)
    [8] Renders 3 angles per scene (BirdEye, POV/Sight/SecurityCam, Wide)
    [9] Saves v12 blend file

  Total output: 4 blend files + 12 renders (3 angles × 4 scenes)
